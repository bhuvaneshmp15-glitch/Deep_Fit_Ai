Rule-based semantic ranker with JD-aware skill weighting. Five scoring components: (1) skill match — 35%, weighted by proficiency and tenure against 35 JD-specific AI skills; (2) experience fit — 25%, combining YOE, title match, and career trajectory; (3) behavioral signals — 20%, using 12 of the 23 Redrob signals including recency, response rate, interview completion, GitHub activity; (4) education — 10%, institution tier and AI field bonus; (5) location — 10%, Pune/Noida exact match preferred. Post-scoring boost layer for retrieval specialists (FAISS/Qdrant/Embeddings 3+ skills) and active candidates. Honeypot penalty for suspicious signal patterns. Runtime ~60s for 100k candidates on CPU.
